About Vision Scholar

As a proud mother of three daughters and first-generation immigrant, Fadia Rostom Makdisi understands education's transformative power from lived experience. She has dedicated her career to empowering others through knowledge, building bridges that unite communities.

With over 30 years' collaborative experience in U.S. and international education across public and private sectors, Fadia is guided by her belief that education is a unifier—transcending cultures to foster shared understanding. A graduate of Damascus University, Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts, and Fitchburg State University, Fadia has served as a professor assistant and principal, inspiring during unprecedented challenges like COVID-19, while ensuring educational excellence and accreditation.

A Berkshire resident by choice, Fadia invests deeply in regional growth as an executive member of the BCC Alumni Board, strengthening the bonds that make the Berkshires a place where all backgrounds can thrive.
