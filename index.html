About

Where Deep Roots Meet Forward Vision

Fadia Rostom is the founder and principal consultant of Vision Scholar Consultation — a K–12 AI education consultancy based in Pittsfield, MA. With over 30 years spanning classroom teaching, school leadership, curriculum development, and technology, she brings a rare combination of technical fluency and educational depth to every engagement.

"AI integration is not about adding tools to classrooms. It's about building the human capacity to use them wisely, equitably, and with purpose."

Before founding Vision Scholar, Fadia served as Principal of Saint Agnes Academy (K–8) in Dalton, MA — where her school earned top marks in NEASC accreditation. She holds two degrees in Computer Science, a Massachusetts Principal/Administrator license, an MA in Educational Leadership from Fitchburg State (2025), and MCSE/MCSA certifications.

As an immigrant who built her life in the US navigating a new language and culture, Fadia brings authentic insight to equity-centered work. She's a mother of three daughters, active in Berkshire community leadership, and chairs The WE in AI Berkshire Summit — a regional convening that has brought together Harvard, MIT, UMass, and DESE partners.

She speaks nationally at conferences including NEASC, DLAC, and MATSOL, and is fluent in Arabic and English with working knowledge of Spanish.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need existing AI expertise to benefit from your programs?
Not at all. We meet you where you are. Our programs are designed for educators at every level — from AI-curious to those already experimenting. We tailor the depth and pace to your team's current knowledge and goals.
How long does transformation typically take?
Most schools see immediate shifts in educator confidence and tool adoption after a single workshop. Sustained change develops over our 90-day implementation support period. The goal is building internal capacity so growth continues long after our formal engagement ends.
What if our faculty is hesitant or skeptical about AI?
That's exactly where this work begins. We address concerns directly, demonstrate practical value through hands-on experience, and honor the professional wisdom educators already bring. Many of our most successful workshops started with skeptical rooms that left energized.
Do you work with public and charter schools, not just independent schools?
Yes. Vision Scholar works with public districts, charter schools, Catholic schools, and independent schools. The work is always adapted to your specific community, constraints, and goals.
What happens after our free consultation?
Within 48 hours, you'll receive a custom proposal outlining recommended services, timeline, and investment. There's no pressure — take the time you need to review with your team. When you're ready, we'll schedule your Discovery & Alignment session and begin.
How do you measure success?
Success metrics are defined together during our Discovery session, based on your goals. Common indicators include educator confidence scores, AI adoption rates, pre/post survey results, and qualitative feedback. We currently see 85–95% adoption rates across workshop participants.
