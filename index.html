About Vision Scholar

At Vision Scholar, we believe learning and technology together unlock new possibilities for personal and professional growth. Founded by Fadia Rostom, a leader with over 30 years of experience supporting individuals, teams, educational institutions, and small businesses, Vision Scholar empowers partners to harness AI and technology to enhance their work and daily lives.

Leadership & Experience

With a rich background in higher education and private school leadership, Fadia combines expertise from both the education sector and the business world. She has designed and delivered professional development programs across cultures and contexts, focusing on practical skills for thriving in today's dynamic, technology-driven environment.

Community Commitment

Fadia is deeply invested in fostering learning communities that connect people, build skills, and inspire innovation to help individuals and organizations adapt and excel in a changing world.
