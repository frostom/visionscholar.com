About Vision Scholar

As a proud mother of three daughters and first-generation immigrant, Fadia Rostom Makdisi understands education's transformative power from lived experience. Having witnessed its life-changing benefits firsthand, she has dedicated her career to empowering others through knowledge and personal growth while building bridges that unite communities.

Community Commitment

A Berkshire resident by choice, Fadia is deeply invested in the region's growth and development.
